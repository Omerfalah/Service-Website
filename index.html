Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

Below you'll find answers to common questions about dental procedures, insurance, and our clinic policies.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine checkup and cleaning. However, some patients may need more frequent visits based on their specific oral health needs.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our office staff will be happy to verify your benefits and help you understand your coverage. We also offer flexible payment options for patients without insurance.

What should I do in case of a dental emergency?

For dental emergencies, please call our office immediately at (555) 123-4567. We reserve time in our schedule for emergency patients and will do our best to see you as soon as possible, often the same day.

Is teeth whitening safe?

When performed by a dental professional, teeth whitening is a safe and effective way to brighten your smile. We use professional-grade whitening products that are much safer and more effective than over-the-counter options.

How can I overcome my fear of dentistry?

We understand dental anxiety is common. Our staff is specially trained to help nervous patients feel comfortable. We offer options like sedation dentistry, entertainment during procedures, and we'll always explain each step so you know what to expect.
